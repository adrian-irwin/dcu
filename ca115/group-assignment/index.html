What Is AIA

AIA Technologies

AIA is an artificial intelligence (AI) app that uses sentiment analysis (the use of natural language processing, computational linguistics, and biometrics to systematically classify, measure, and extract subjective knowledge) in order to make decisions and provide personalized experiences for users to construct complex and meaningful conversations.


Sentiment Analysis employs machine learning methods to determine how a person is feeling in order to make a more human-like conversation. Normally, sentiment analysis relies solely on text, but AIA incorporates data from the camera and microphone to make AIA more personalized for the user.
